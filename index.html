Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the hackathon?

Our hackathons are open to participants of all skill levels, from beginners to experts. Students, professionals, and enthusiasts are all welcome to join!

Do I need to have a team before registering?

No, you can register as an individual and form a team during our team formation session at the beginning of the event. Of course, you're also welcome to register with a pre-formed team.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in our hackathons is completely free. We believe in making innovation accessible to everyone.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

You should bring your laptop, charger, any specialized hardware you might need for your project, and your creativity! We'll provide food, drinks, and a comfortable workspace.

Are there any restrictions on project topics?

Each hackathon has specific themes and challenge tracks, but you're generally free to work on any project that excites you. The only requirement is that all code must be written during the hackathon.
